Ground Floor

On entering the villa you will find yourself in a spacious hallway with high ceilings, immediately feeling the luxury, space and coolness of the villa. The ground floor is completely open plan, leading to a fully equipped kitchen / dining area on your right, and a large lounge area on your left, for relaxing in the evenings. From the kitchen you have direct access to your private swimming pool, balcony area for barbeques and watching the sunsets after a hard day by your pool, and also the stunning manicured gardens. The ground floor also offers a family WC as well as a small stairway to the upper and lower floors.

Uper Floor

There are three bedrooms on the upper floor, as well as a balcony overlooking the ground floor, and a family bathroom with full bath. The master bedroom has a double sized bed, walk-in wardrobe and is en-suite, with a walk-in shower. The second bedroom has 2 single sized beds and fitted wardrobes as well as a shared balcony with views. The third bedroom has a double bed and shared balcony and views. Designed with your comfort and living needs in mind, Villa Nickalos is fully equipped and promises to fulfil all of your expectations, and provide you with an exceptional, and unforgettable holiday experience.

There are a couple of steps for entry to the villa, but all bedrooms and bathrooms are accessed by a very small stairwell. Villa Nichalos is suitable for people with mild mobility difficulties. The doors are normal doors and would not pass a wheelchair.

The beautiful beach resort of Coral Bay is often described as the best bay on the west of the island. And it’s no surprise, when you consider the calm turquoise sea, dipping gently into some of the clearest off shore diving space in the Mediterranean, the scattered palm trees, the 600 meters of soft white sand and, in summertime, the almost cloudless blue skies. Coral Bay is a 5 minute walk from the villa.

Akamas National Park

Located in the west of Cyprus the Akamas National Park is possibly the most picturesque corner of the island. Covering approximately 230 square kilometers the Akamas peninsula is where you will find wild landscape, amazing natural beauty, deep gorges, and wide sandy bays. The diversity of wildlife is of outstanding ecological importance for the Mediterranean and the wide variety of fauna is certainly impressive. Here you can find 168 different varieties of birds, 12 different mammals, 20 different reptiles and 16 different species of butterfly.
The importance of the Akamas Peninsula is recognized far beyond the shores of Cyprus. The European Council has included the Akamas Peninsula within its Mediterranean protection programme and within Cyprus; Friends of the Earth and Greenpeace are campaigning hard for the Akamas Peninsula to be declared a National Park.
