Cyprus Entry Requirements by Category

The health ministry ranks countries based on the weekly evaluation of the ECDC. In cases of countries not assessed by the ECDC, this evaluation is done by the health ministry.

Vaccinated travelers arriving into Cyprus who have received both shots, with the 2nd a minimum of 7 days before arrival, are free to enter with no restrictions whatsoever. Proof of vaccination is required.

Green category travellers have no restrictions.

Orange category require a negative PCR test certificate not older than 72 hours prior to departure. No self-isolation measures apply in this category.

Red category regions require two negative PCR tests, one within 72 hours before departure and another upon arrival in Cyprus. No self-isolation measures apply in this category.

Grey, or special permit category, countries, must submit a negative PCR test not older than 72 hours before departure and must self-isolate upon arrival in accordance with the instructions of the health ministry.

Laboratory testing of minors under the age of 12 for COVID-19, prior to departure or upon arrival in the Republic of Cyprus, is not required. It should be noted that the acquisition of CyprusFlightPass continues to be mandatory for these minors as well.
